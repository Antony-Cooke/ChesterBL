ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING

The Annual General Meeting took place on Tuesday 16th July 2019 at Waverton MI, at 7.30pm.

At this meeting, it was acknowledged that the EABA now use spotted balls in all their competitions and Chester Billiards League accept their use.

A proposal to abandon the "5 up, 5 down" handicap sysytem was defeated and remains in place.

The "3 man rule" was rescinded - see League Constitution for details of this rule revision.

Handicap meeting to take place on 20th August 2019 at 7:30pm at Waverton Institute.

The 2019-2020 League Season to Commence on 17th September.

At present we have 8 registered teams.

Fixture list to be published on this website shortly.

Tony Cooke will be putting together Team packs and handbooks prior to the Handicap Meeting on the 20th of August.

ANNUAL PRESENTATION EVENING
The Annual Presentation Evening has been arranged for Tuesday 21st May 2019 at Waverton MI, Starting at 7.30pm

04/03/2019
Waverton A beat Chester Cons 10-0 in the play off for the League title

Congratulations Waverton A on becoming our League Champions

24/04/2019
Buckley Cons win the Cayzer Cup
Chester Cons are runners up.

Most Wins: Geoff Brassington (Chester Cons) 11 wins
Highest Break: 138 Geoff Brassington (Chester Cons)

01/05/2019
Geoff Brassington wins Davies Cup 400 - 66
Geoff's victory included his 9th break over 100 this season plus 3 other breaks over 50
Colin Jones is the runner up, for the second year running.

07/05/2019
Geoff Brassington wins Will Thomas Cup 200 - 103
Geoff's victory included his 10th and 11th breaks over 100, 143 and 103 unfinished to cap off a prolific season
Mike Bennett is the runner up.

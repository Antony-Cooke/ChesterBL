Dress Code
Now that we are at the Semi-Final stage of the competition, please note that the dress code comes into operation.
No Jeans, trainers or collarless shirts please.
In the event of a failure to comply with this rule, the offending player will forfeit his game and his opponent will score 200 points.
The offending player will score the amount of his handicap.
